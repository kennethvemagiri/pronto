Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

pronto ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at pronto.online. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the service:

  • Prompts you submit — the text you type into the workbench is sent to OpenAI's API to generate a polished prompt. We do not permanently store your prompt text.
  • IP address — used solely to enforce rate limits (20 requests per hour on the free tier). Not stored beyond the current session window.
  • Contact information — if you email us, we retain your email address and message to respond to you.

2. How we use your information

  • To deliver the prompt polishing service via OpenAI's API.
  • To prevent abuse through IP-based rate limiting.
  • To respond to your enquiries and support requests.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-party services

Your prompt text is processed by OpenAI to generate results. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We recommend not submitting sensitive or confidential information in your prompts.

4. Cookies

pronto does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We do not use advertising or retargeting technologies.

5. Data retention

We do not persistently store prompt content. Contact enquiries are retained only as long as necessary to resolve your request.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@pronto.online.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@pronto.online.
